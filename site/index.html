Wireless Networking Researcher • November 2012 - January 2015

I was on the Malawi TV White Spaces research team. We did research on the viability of using TV White Spaces for wireless networks and Internet. TV White Spaces are unused frequencies in the TV band. Since TV frequencies have high wavelength (they are low frequencies), they can be transimitted over long distances without relays; offering a low cost opportunity for Wide Area Wireless Networks.

My duties included: LINUX Bash & C programming for radio signal analysis, Python Network Programming, HTML5 & Javascript for Data Visualization, Network monitoring, making presentation on TVWS technology as well as co-authoring research papers.
